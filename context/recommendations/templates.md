# Recommendation Templates

## Purpose
Structured templates for generating specific, actionable recommendations with outreach targets based on audit findings. Replaces generic recommendations like "establish Wikipedia presence" with concrete actions.

## Philosophy (from Rokas feedback)
- Recommendations should be **achievable** and **realistic**
- Include **outreach targets** with contact information when possible
- Reference **specific competitor content** that drove their visibility
- Avoid expensive/impractical suggestions (Wikipedia costs $100s-$1000s)
- Focus on **data-driven** actions based on what sources LLMs actually cite

---

## Recommendation Structure

Each recommendation should include:

```
### Priority [N]: [Action Title]

**Evidence:**
- [Specific finding from audit that drives this recommendation]
- [Competitor X has Y that brand lacks]
- [Source Z cited N times across queries]

**Action:**
1. [Step 1]
2. [Step 2]
3. [Step 3]

**Outreach Target:** (if applicable)
- Site: [URL]
- Author/Contact: [Name]
- LinkedIn: [Profile URL if found]
- Approach: [Brief script/angle]

**Effort:** [Low/Medium/High] ([estimated hours])
**Expected Impact:** [Specific metric improvement]
**Timeline:** [Weeks to completion]
```

## Template 1: Missing Review Platform Presence

### Priority [N]: Establish [Platform] Profile

**Evidence:**
- Competitors [A, B, C] all have [Platform] profiles with 50+ reviews
- [Platform] cited in [X/8] queries across LLMs
- Brand currently has no presence on [Platform]

**Action:**
1. Create verified company profile on [Platform]
2. Add complete product information, pricing, screenshots
3. Request reviews from existing customers (5-10 initial reviews)
4. Respond to all reviews professionally

**Outreach Target:**
- Site: [Platform URL]
- Contact: Self-service registration
- Approach: Follow platform guidelines for profile creation

**Effort:** Low (2-4 hours setup + ongoing)
**Expected Impact:** Appear in evaluative queries; increase citation rate by 15-25%
**Timeline:** 1-2 weeks for profile; 4-6 weeks for review volume

## Template 2: Competitor Article Gap

### Priority [N]: Create Article Similar to [Competitor URL]

**Evidence:**
- [Competitor article URL] cited [X] times across [Y/8] queries
- Article covers: [topic summary]
- Brand has no equivalent content

**Action:**
1. Analyze competitor article structure and topics covered
2. Create comprehensive alternative (2,000+ words)
3. Include unique data, case studies, or insights competitor lacks
4. Add schema markup for FAQ, HowTo, or Article
5. Promote through existing channels

**Outreach Target:**
- N/A (content creation, not outreach)

**Effort:** Medium (8-16 hours)
**Expected Impact:** Compete for same query types; potential citation in comparative queries
**Timeline:** 2-3 weeks for creation and indexing

## Template 3: Blog Mention Request

### Priority [N]: Request Inclusion in [Article Title]

**Evidence:**
- [Article URL] mentions competitors [A, B, C] but not brand
- Article cited in [X/8] queries
- Article is a "Best [category]" or comparison piece

**Action:**
1. Identify article author via byline or LinkedIn search
2. Send personalized outreach (see script below)
3. Offer: unique data, expert quote, or case study
4. Follow up after 5-7 days if no response

**Outreach Target:**
- Site: [Publication URL]
- Article: [Article URL]
- Author: [Author Name]
- LinkedIn: [Author LinkedIn URL]
- Email: [If findable, or use LinkedIn message]

**Outreach Script:**
```
Hi [Author Name],

I noticed your excellent article on [topic] - really comprehensive coverage of the space.

I'm reaching out from [Brand]. We're in the [category] space and noticed we weren't included in your comparison. Would you be open to adding us?

I can provide:
- [Unique data point about product/service]
- [Customer case study or testimonial]
- [Expert quote on industry trend]

Happy to provide whatever info would be most useful for your readers.

Thanks,
[Your name]
```

**Effort:** Low (1-2 hours per outreach)
**Expected Impact:** Direct citation in high-authority source
**Timeline:** 1-4 weeks depending on author responsiveness

## Template 4: Link Exchange Opportunity

### Priority [N]: Propose Link Exchange with [Site/Author]

**Evidence:**
- [Site/Author] writes about [category] and mentions competitors
- Their content cited by LLMs [X] times
- Brand's blog mentions related topics but doesn't link to them

**Action:**
1. Identify mutual benefit opportunity
2. Propose: "We mention your tool in our article, you add us to yours"
3. Create genuine value (don't just ask for link)

**Outreach Target:**
- Site: [URL]
- Contact: [Author/Editor Name]
- LinkedIn: [Profile URL]

**Outreach Script:**
```
Hi [Name],

Love your work on [topic]. We recently wrote about [related topic] and referenced your insights.

Would you be interested in a content collaboration? Specifically:
- We can add [their brand/product] to our [your article] with context about [use case]
- In exchange, we'd love to be considered for your [their article]

No pressure - just thought there might be mutual value here.

Best,
[Your name]
```

**Effort:** Low (1-2 hours per exchange)
**Expected Impact:** Mutual citation boost; improved cross-link signals
**Timeline:** 2-4 weeks

## Template 5: Industry Publication Pitch

### Priority [N]: Pitch [Publication] for Coverage

**Evidence:**
- [Publication] cited [X] times in LLM responses
- Competitors have been featured recently
- Brand has newsworthy angle: [funding, launch, milestone]

**Action:**
1. Identify appropriate editor/reporter
2. Craft pitch around newsworthy hook
3. Include data, quotes, and visual assets
4. Follow up professionally

**Outreach Target:**
- Publication: [Name]
- Reporter: [Name] (covers [beat])
- Email: [Journalist email]
- Twitter/X: [@handle]

**Pitch Structure:**
```
Subject: [Newsworthy hook] - [Brand] [Achievement/Launch]

Hi [Reporter Name],

[Opening hook with news value]

Key points:
- [Data point 1]
- [Data point 2]
- [Unique angle]

Available for interview: [Executive name, title]

Assets: [Screenshots, data visualizations, customer quotes]

[Your name]
[Contact info]
```

**Effort:** Medium (4-8 hours for pitch + follow-up)
**Expected Impact:** High-authority citation; brand awareness
**Timeline:** 2-8 weeks (editorial cycles vary)

## Template 7: Reddit/Community Engagement

### Priority [N]: Establish Presence in [Subreddit/Community]

**Evidence:**
- [r/subreddit] content cited in [X] queries
- Competitors actively participate and are mentioned
- Brand has no visible presence

**Action:**
1. Create authentic account (not overtly branded)
2. Provide value first - answer questions, share insights
3. Mention brand only when genuinely relevant
4. Build reputation over time (1+ months)

**Outreach Target:**
- Subreddit: r/[name]
- Key threads: [URLs where competitors mentioned]

**Guidelines:**
- DO: Provide genuine expertise, help users
- DON'T: Spam, over-promote, or astroturf
- Build karma before mentioning brand

**Effort:** Low-Medium (ongoing 1-2 hours/week)
**Expected Impact:** Community trust signal; organic mentions
**Timeline:** 4-8 weeks for visible presence

## Recommendations to AVOID

Per Rokas feedback, these are impractical for most brands:

| Recommendation | Why to Avoid | Alternative |
|---------------|--------------|-------------|
| "Create Wikipedia article" | $100s-$1000s, requires agencies, may be rejected | Focus on Crunchbase, industry directories |
| "Get featured on TechCrunch" | Requires newsworthy hook, very competitive | Target tier-2 industry publications |
| "Build knowledge graph presence" | Abstract, hard to action | Focus on structured data, directory listings |
| "Improve brand authority" | Vague, not actionable | Specific actions: reviews, mentions, content |

## Priority Scoring Matrix

When generating recommendations, score based on:

| Factor | Low (1) | Medium (2) | High (3) |
|--------|---------|------------|----------|
| Impact | Marginal improvement | Noticeable improvement | Significant ranking change |
| Effort | 10+ hours | 4-10 hours | 1-4 hours |
| Speed | 8+ weeks | 2-8 weeks | 1-2 weeks |
| Confidence | Speculative | Based on patterns | Direct evidence from audit |

**Priority Score = (Impact Ã— Confidence) / Effort**
